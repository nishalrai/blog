Use of Internet

It’s a very interesting fact and surprise to know the impact of internet in these days. Our daily life is depending on desires and goals due to the extensive use of internet. It’s been a universal fact about the both positive and negative impact of every aspects. However, the positive impact has boomed the humanity development in a great extent. While there are various uses of the Internet but you can use the internet for getting an online education. We can use the Internet to promote your business online. We can do online courses and improve your writing, communication, business and online marketing skills. Online shopping, social media, emails, chatting are common things that we do daily. The global network of computers has changed our lives tremendously. We are hungry to use the Internet. That’s why more than 40% of the world’s population is connected to the Internet according to internetlivestatus.com.
We, usually start our day after notifications and emails. This means that as soon as we wake up we are flooded with information from different sources. Sometimes, it is struggling to prioritize and decide which information is useful and which is not. Using the Internet positively means we can decide what is important for the day. The Internet has made life a lot easier by making information more accessible to all and creating connections with different people around the world. However, it has also led a lot of people to spend too much time in front of the computer, so much so that it becomes the center of their lives. This can lead to an Internet or computer addiction. Howevr, in case of it's advantages cannot be missed.
One of the positives that technology provides is often found in the classroom to assist learning. Traditional classrooms have a chalkboard where a teacher delivers lessons and instructions to students. Technology in the classroom has manifested itself so that teachers can use a whiteboard to teach students. These whiteboards allow teachers to call up presentations for their class. Instead of using chalk as one would do on a chalkboard, a teacher simply needs to wipe away whatever is written with a standard eraser or a damp cloth and then start teaching another subject. Other common examples of technology in the classroom come from the computers the students use to do class work and homework. They can communicate with their teachers away from school by using email, text messaging and video calling.
